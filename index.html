About Me

1987

Born

I was born in Dayton, Ohio on Tuesday August 25th, 1987. My family lived in the small town of Brookville, Ohio where corn fields and sky were all you could see for miles.

2004

My Introduction to Web Design

During my last two years of high school I joined a program at a technical school to study interactive-multimedia. Here I was introduced to basic web-design skills using HTML, Adobe: Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.

2017

College Graduation

In 2017 I graduated from Portland State University with a Bachelors of Science in Women, Gender, and Sexuality Studies and a minor in Philosophy. My focus during these years was on how I could re-build an Intro to Ethics Philosophy course to include the perspectives of groups of people who have been historically left out, and ensure those same people have access to higher education.

2018

Teaching English Abroad

In 2018 I received my Teaching English as a Foreign Language certificate and set off for Costa Rica for a new career as an english teacher. This was a huge learning experience and taught me many valuable lessons. Such as: Humidity is not so great, english is an ever-changing language, and travelling the world is my new favorite hobby.

2021

Lets Become a DEV

In 2021 I enrolled in a coding bootcamp at Epicodus and began my six month journey to becoming a real life computer programmer. While the learning curve is high, I love the process of building applications and learning new programming languages.
